Pokémon Listings

Like most Pokémon games, the goal of Pokémon GO is to capture all of the Pokémon. This is done by finding them in various locations around the globe. As citing location for each Pokémon is highly illogical due to the vast number of possible locations around the globe, this page details with the vital statistics of each of the Pokémon in the game.

Height and weight of each Pokémon is the standard height and weight, but in this game that varies . We also list the maximum Combat Points which can be obtained by the Pokémon if it has good IVs and is at the max level.

Navigate by:
Hit Points | Attack | Defense

For more in-depth details on each Pokémon, click their image or name.
